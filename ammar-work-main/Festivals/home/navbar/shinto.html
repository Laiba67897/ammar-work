Shinto Festivals

Shinto, the indigenous religion of Japan, has a rich tapestry of festivals, known as matsuri, which are celebrated throughout the year. These festivals are deeply rooted in Japanese culture and traditions, often centering around the veneration of kami (spirits or deities) and the natural world.
