Christianity Festivals

Christianity, one of the world's largest religions, celebrates several festivals throughout the liturgical year, each with its own theological significance, customs, and traditions. These festivals commemorate key events in the life of Jesus Christ and the history of Christianity, providing opportunities for worship, reflection, and community gathering.
